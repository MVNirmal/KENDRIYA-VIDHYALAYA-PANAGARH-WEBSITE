KENDRIYA VIDHYALAYA PANAGARH

Kendriya Vidyalaya Panagarh Durgapur, is a Senior Secondary School (XI-XII), affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).

The School is a Co-ed Day School, with classes from I to XII. It is an Hindi, English Medium school.

The school is located in Panagarh area of Durgapur. Kendriya Vidyalaya Panagarh was established in 1966. It is a Government School and is part of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) and is managed by Defence.
